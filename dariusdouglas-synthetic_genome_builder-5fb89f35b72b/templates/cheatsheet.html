Bioinformatics Cheat Sheet

Term Definition
Nucleotide A compound consisting of a nucleotide linked to a phosphate group. Nucleotides form the basic structural unit of nucleic acids such as DNA.
Amino Acid Organic compounds that make up proteins
Genome An organism's complete set of genes
Transcription First step of gene expression, in which a particular segment of DNA is copied into RNA (mRNA) by the enzyme RNA polymerase
Translation Translation is the process in which cellular ribosomes create proteins
